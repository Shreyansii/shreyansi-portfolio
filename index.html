A bit about me

Hi I'm Shreyansi, and I am a creative who loves playing with colours, designs and stories, and to create things. I am a final year Computer Engineering student at Kathmandu Engineering College { TU, IOE }.

Currently I am honing my skills in full-stack development whilst smoothening my design concepts with every project I work on, I believe it also helps me approach problems from different angles. My interest primarily lies in cognitive science and immersive art experience( integration of art & culture with tech).

Apart from coding & designing, I am a visual artist (photography + video editing) with an experience of more than 4 years as well, I love photographing stuffs the independent journalism way, getting fascinated by stories in everything, metaphysical filmmaking, creative & curatorial writing, and that has helped me research practically and more lively about the innovative-tech platform I want to create for art & artists, by understanding the need and scenario from an artist perspective as well as a technical person.
